## What is Genny?

Genny is a **paper trading platform** that lets you practice trading Solana memecoins without risking real money. Experience the thrill of memecoin trading with virtual SOL, track your performance on leaderboards, and learn trading strategies in a safe environment.

## How It Works

Genny simulates the complete memecoin trading experience:

1. **Install Extension**: Add Genny to Chrome for instant trading access
2. **Link Account**: Connect your profile to track trades and stats
3. **Start Trading**: Buy and sell memecoins with virtual SOL balance
4. **Track Performance**: Monitor P&L, win rate, and trading volume
5. **Compete**: Climb the leaderboards and showcase your skills

<Note>
  **Educational Purpose Only**: Genny is for learning and entertainment. All trading is simulated with virtual currency.
</Note>
