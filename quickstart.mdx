## Start Trading in 3 Steps

Get Genny up and running and start paper trading memecoins immediately.

### Step 1: Install the Chrome Extension

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="download" title="Download from Chrome Web Store">
    1. Visit the [Genny Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/genny-paper-trading-for-me/extension-id-placeholder) in the Chrome Web Store
    2. Click "Add to Chrome" to install the extension
    3. Pin the extension to your toolbar for easy access

    <Tip>The extension works on pump.fun and other memecoin trading sites.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shield-check" title="Grant Permissions">
    When prompted, allow the extension to:
    - Read and change site data on pump.fun
    - Access your browsing history (for trading context only)

    <Warning>Genny never accesses your real wallets or funds. All trading is simulated.</Warning>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Create Your Profile

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-plus" title="Link Your Account">
    1. Click the Genny extension icon in your browser
    2. Click "Get Funded" to visit genny.lol
    3. Enter a username and click "Link Account"
    4. Check your email for a verification code
    5. Enter the code to complete account linking

    <Tip>Optional: Connect your X (Twitter) account for social features and profile pictures.</Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Start Paper Trading

<Accordion icon="play" title="Make Your First Trade">
  1. Visit [pump.fun](https://pump.fun) in your browser
  2. Look for the Genny trading panel on any memecoin page
  3. Click "Buy" or "Sell" with your virtual SOL balance
  4. Watch your portfolio grow on the leaderboard!

  <Note>You start with 1,000 virtual SOL to practice trading.</Note>
</Accordion>
