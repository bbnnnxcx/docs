## Profile Setup

### Account Linking
1. **Create Profile**: Visit genny.lol and enter a username
2. **Verify Email**: Receive and enter verification code
3. **Link Extension**: Connect your browser extension to your profile

### X (Twitter) Integration
- **Optional Connection**: Link your X account for enhanced features
- **Profile Picture**: Display your X avatar on leaderboards
- **Social Sharing**: Share trading achievements

<Note>
  X integration is optional but recommended for the full social experience.
</Note>

## Profile Features

### Public Information
- **Username**: Your display name on leaderboards
- **Avatar**: X profile picture (if connected)
- **Trading Stats**: Public performance metrics
- **Rankings**: Current leaderboard positions

### Private Dashboard
- **Detailed Stats**: Complete trading history
- **Portfolio View**: Current holdings and values
- **Performance Charts**: P&L over time
- **Trade Analysis**: Win/loss patterns

## Social Features

### Community Interaction
- **Leaderboard Competition**: Compete with other traders
- **Achievement Sharing**: Post trading milestones
- **Strategy Discussion**: Share trading approaches

### Privacy Controls
- **Public Profile**: Control what information is visible
- **Anonymous Trading**: Trade without social features
- **Data Control**: Manage your personal information

## Profile Management

### Updating Information
- **Change Username**: Update display name anytime
- **Reconnect X**: Refresh social media connection
- **Reset Balance**: Request balance reset for fresh start

### Account Security
- **Email Verification**: Secure account access
- **Extension Linking**: Unique connection codes
- **Data Privacy**: No sensitive information stored

## Achievement System

### Trading Milestones
- **First Trade**: Complete your initial transaction
- **Profit Target**: Reach specific P&L thresholds
- **Win Streak**: Consecutive profitable trades
- **Volume Goals**: Trading volume achievements

### Social Recognition
- **Leaderboard Rankings**: Top positions in categories
- **Community Votes**: Popular trading strategies
- **Consistency Awards**: Regular trading activity

<Warning>
  **Data Privacy**: Genny respects your privacy. Profile data is used only for platform features and never sold to third parties.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Extension Not Linking**: Clear browser cache and retry
- **Stats Not Updating**: Refresh leaderboard page
- **X Connection Failed**: Re-authorize social media access

### Support
- **Contact**: Reach out for profile assistance
- **Data Export**: Request your data anytime
- **Account Deletion**: Remove your profile completely

<Note>
  Your profile data helps improve the Genny experience for all users while maintaining your privacy and security.
</Note>
